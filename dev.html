For the Developer

Open Package Manager, aims to help those developers who maintain linux packages, but don't have the time or energy to write a full blown android application.

A typical example of such an application would be nmap. Nmap currently has an Android support page but they do not have an entire Android application yet. While they develop the Android application, they could continue to distribute their binaries that are cross compiled to Andriod such that people can still enjoy using it, and that is what we offer.

Current steps to publish Linux applications in the Android ecosystem

  • Cross-compile Binaries

  • Develop Android Application

  • Publish Application on PlayStore

What we offer

  • Cross-compile Binaries

  • Publish Application on Open Package Manager


We'd like to try to make a developers life a little easier.
